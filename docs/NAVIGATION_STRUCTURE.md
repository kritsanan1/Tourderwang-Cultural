# Phu Thai Cultural Heritage Website - Navigation Structure

## Overview
This document outlines the comprehensive navigation structure for the Phu Thai cultural heritage website, designed to serve multiple user types while preserving and promoting authentic cultural experiences.

## Primary Navigation Categories

### 1. Cultural Heritage (มรดกทางวัฒนธรรม)
**Purpose**: Explore authentic Phu Thai traditions, history, and cultural practices preserved through generations.
**Target Users**: Tourists, Locals, Researchers
**Icon**: BookOpen

#### Subcategories:
- **Phu Thai History** - Rich historical narrative and migration stories
- **Traditional Festivals** - Vibrant celebrations and seasonal ceremonies  
- **Local Cuisine** - Authentic recipes and culinary traditions
- **Arts & Crafts** - Traditional textiles, pottery, and artistic expressions
- **Cultural Sites** - Temples, museums, and heritage locations
- **Virtual Tours** - Immersive 360° experiences of cultural landmarks
- **Language & Dialect** - Phu Thai language basics and linguistic heritage

### 2. Community Hub (ศูนย์กลางชุมชน)
**Purpose**: Connect with locals, participate in community activities, and access local services.
**Target Users**: Locals, Tourists, Business Owners
**Icon**: Users

#### Subcategories:
- **Job Opportunities** - Employment listings in Wang Sam Mo district
- **Local Events** - Community gatherings and cultural activities
- **Community Forum** - Discussion platform for community engagement
- **Volunteer Programs** - Cultural preservation and community development
- **Community News** - Latest updates and developments

### 3. Local Business (ธุรกิจท้องถิ่น)
**Purpose**: Discover authentic local businesses and support the community economy.
**Target Users**: Tourists, Locals, Business Owners
**Icon**: Store

#### Subcategories:
- **Business Directory** - Verified local businesses and services
- **Accommodations** - Homestays, guesthouses, and hotels
- **Restaurants & Food** - Local eateries and traditional cuisine
- **Artisan Shops** - Handmade crafts and traditional products
- **Tour Services** - Guided tours and experience packages
- **List Your Business** - Business registration and promotion

### 4. Explore & Visit (สำรวจและเยี่ยมชม)
**Purpose**: Plan visits with interactive maps, travel guides, and local recommendations.
**Target Users**: Tourists, Researchers
**Icon**: Map

#### Subcategories:
- **Interactive Map** - Detailed navigation with cultural points of interest
- **Travel Guide** - Comprehensive travel information and local customs
- **Suggested Itineraries** - Pre-planned cultural tours and routes
- **Photo Gallery** - Stunning photography of natural beauty and culture
- **Visitor Reviews** - Authentic experiences from previous visitors
- **Seasonal Highlights** - Best times to visit for festivals and activities

## User Type Configurations

### Tourist Focus
- **Primary Sections**: Culture, Explore, Business
- **Featured Content**: Virtual tours, interactive map, accommodations
- **Call-to-Actions**: "Plan Your Visit", "Book Tours", "Find Accommodations"

### Local Community Focus
- **Primary Sections**: Community, Business, Culture
- **Featured Content**: Job portal, local events, community forum
- **Call-to-Actions**: "Find Jobs", "Join Community", "List Business"

### Researcher Focus
- **Primary Sections**: Culture, Research Resources, Media Center
- **Featured Content**: Historical archives, cultural sites, research materials
- **Call-to-Actions**: "Access Research", "Download Resources", "Collaborate"

### Business Owner Focus
- **Primary Sections**: Business, Community, Partnerships
- **Featured Content**: Business registration, directory, networking events
- **Call-to-Actions**: "List Business", "Join Network", "Partner With Us"

## Mobile-Friendly Navigation Principles

1. **Simplified Structure**: Maximum 5 primary items in mobile navigation
2. **Touch-Friendly**: Minimum 44px touch targets
3. **Progressive Disclosure**: Expandable sections to manage complexity
4. **Contextual Actions**: Location-based and user-type specific shortcuts
5. **Offline Capability**: Essential navigation available offline
